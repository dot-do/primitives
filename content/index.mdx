## Why MDX & MDX-LD?

MDX brings together structured data, unstructured content, executable code, and UI components. [MDX.org.AI](https://mdx.org.ai) extends this foundation with AI-native types, while [MDXLD.org](https://mdxld.org) provides the linked data infrastructure.
